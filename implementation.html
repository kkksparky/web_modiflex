Execution Plan

36-Month Delivery Path from TRL 5 to TRL 7

The implementation model combines R&D validation, pilot deployment, and industrialization preparation to generate both technical evidence and replication readiness for follow-on scale-up.

Programme Timeline

M1-M16 | Platform R&D and Integration
Motor module, ESC variants, casting interfaces, and reducer development with bench validation.
M12-M30 | Pilot Demonstrations
Site deployment in Norway and Bulgaria with baseline and operational performance measurement.
M24-M36 | Industrialization Preparation
Pre-compliance, supply-chain planning, and commercial transition roadmap for post-LIFE scale.

Milestone-Oriented Governance

Key Milestones

  • M5: single module validation complete.
  • M12: integrated stack-housing-reducer lab validation.
  • M16: pilot units installed, TRL transition phase active.
  • M34-M36: final validation and next-phase roadmap completion.

Risk-Control Logic

  • Thermal and dynamic stacking risks managed through simulation and test loops.
  • Reducer durability risk managed through material and life-cycle validation.
  • Deployment timing risk mitigated with phased pilot scheduling and buffer logic.
