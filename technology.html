Technology Platform

One Mechanical Logic, Multiple Power Levels

MODYFLEX combines stackable axial-flux motor units, shared-diameter ESC modules, and a modular planetary reducer family to cover broad duty cycles without redesigning the complete drive.

Core Architecture

Motor Module

  • Air-core axial-flux module as the base building block.
  • 48V and 100V variants with shared mechanical components.
  • Linear stacking from 1 to 5 modules supports incremental scaling.

ESC Family

  • ESC-2 for 1-2 modules and ESC-5 for 1-5 modules.
  • Integrated encoder pathway via central shaft bore.
  • Designed for closed-loop control and synchronized operation.

Reducer Family

  • Shared form factor with motor and controller interfaces.
  • Ratio range from 1:3 to 1:50 for high flexibility.
  • Supports compact high-torque deployment scenarios.
